Cameras

Canon T5i (April 2013)

This is the camera that I use for "high quality" YouTube videos and school events.

Fujifilm Finepix S5200

This is an old camera that I used, it takes good photos, but the videos are in 480p. I still have this $300-ish camera in my room.

Sony FX3 (February 2021)

This is a modern cool camera that I saw online, many YouTubers shout it out. I don't have it and it costs nearly $4,000.

Sony DSC-H70 (January 2011)

I got this camera from my aunt. I used this camera to hide in my pocket from time to time. Despite being from 2011, the pictures it takes are pretty decent as well as the 720p video.

Panasonic HC-VX1 (January 2018)

This is a random camcorder I found online, I wish I had this one, I use a Polaroid camcorder at home and it sucks. This camera costs nearly $800 and shoots great 4K footage.

Insta 360 One X2

Insta 360 is a camera that YouTubers sponsor a lot. It's a small 360 camera that records from every angle and the quality of it is pretty good. It's a cool device, but I'm not sure if I want one.
