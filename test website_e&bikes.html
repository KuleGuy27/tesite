Bicycles and Electric Ones

Infinity Boss Three

I got this bike from my other aunt's house, since no one uses it, she gave it to me. I use this bike pretty regularly for leisure or to go to school, the bike is great!

Mongoose Ledge 2.1 Pink

I got this bike from my aunt's house. 1st bike I got introduced to. I use this bike once in a blue moon, it's for short people and I don't like the seat.

AM-ZNH-02-26 Ebike

This is my first ebike I ever got. I use this regularly on roads to go to Walmart or trail to school. It's kinda fast class 2 ebike but the range is not as long.

Philodo H8 Dual Motor Fat Tire Ebike

I might buy this, it looks cool and powerful (35MPH). It even has an LCD screen and a phone holder! This is the 1st dual motor ebike I found on YouTube. I'm not sure if it's legal to ride one on the road though...

Async A1 Pro

I found this futuristic looking ebike because of TailHappyTV on YouTube. It's the same as the Philodo at 35MPH, but the range is 150 miles!!! It has a big battery, one motor (jeez), a throttle, and an LCD screen.

Segway Emoped C80

This cool looking ebike looks more like a moped, but it has pedals to make it legally an ebike. The bike looks great but is slow. It's slow at climbing hills and actually goes 19 instead of the marketed 20mph. This "ebike" is more of a mobility scooter and it's something I don't take seriously.
