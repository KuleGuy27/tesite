Cool Motorcyles

Vespa GTS 300

This scooter (yes, scooters are a type of motorcycle) is an electric rendition of the classic Vespa. It costs $7,999 USD, goes 45 mph, has 124 miles of range, and takes 4 hours for a full charge.

CSC Monterey

This scooter is an electric rendition of the Honda Super Cub, the most sold motor vehicle in the world. It costs $2,395 USD, goes 32 mph, has 20-60 miles of range, and takes 7 hours for a full charge.

CSC RX1E

This motorcycle is a great "budget" commuter vehicle if you want to cruise around your city. It costs $7,988 USD, goes 80 mph, has 112 miles of range, and takes 6 hours for a full charge.

Honda Super Cub C125 2023

This is the most sold motor vehicle in the world, this is the 125cc version of it. It costs $3,849 USD, goes 65 mph, has 150 miles of range, and it uses 1.0 gallon fuel tank to run.

Sondors Metacycle

I found this moto on YouTube. The design is cool/simplistic and it goes pretty fast. It costs $6,500 USD, goes 80 mph, has 80 miles of range, and takes 5.5 hours for a full charge.

Zero DSRX

I've never heard of this before but it's because talked about a lot. I guess it's because of the price and specs. The moto costs $19,995 USD, goes 112 mph, has 180 miles of range, and takes 1 hour for a full charge.
